Polish couch talks is one of the events accompanying the anti-racism week organized by the Human Rights Association in Iceland (see details here ) The aim of the project is to encourage conversation about Polish culture and the life of Poles in Iceland – our daily experiences and observations- all this in a number of stories , each coming from another´s perspective. Would you like to join in?

Polish couch talks was an ev for little talks. If you're interested in Polish culture or you want to know how the reality of life in Iceland looks like for the Polish community or maybe you just want to hear personal stories of participants, you are welcome to share a couch with us.
