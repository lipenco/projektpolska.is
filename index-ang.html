Our Mission

Projekt Polska is a community based project aimed at building a cultural dialogue between Iceland and Poland. Association Projek Polska was established in October 2012 as an initiative of several Poles associated with Iceland – people who feel the need to contribute to both communities.

Planning on a bright shared future

Supporting independent ideas

Promoting intercultural understanding
